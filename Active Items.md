There are TWO types of Active Items:

## Consumables
**Consumable** Active Items are destroyed upon use, however may be stacked and used as many times as you can during a turn

## Non-Consumables
**Non-consumable** items can be used as many times as you want but must be recharged between uses 

## Scrolls
**Scrolls** are Active Items that do NOTHING in battle. They can only be used at the [[Merchants#Shrine Maiden|Shrine Maiden]] or [[Merchants#Blacksmith|Blacksmith]] to apply an [[Weapons#Attributes|Attribute]] to a [[Weapons]]
